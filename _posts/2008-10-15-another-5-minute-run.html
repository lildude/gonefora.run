I went for my 2nd 5 min @ 2% @ 6mph run on the treadmill this afternoon, after my weight lifting session, and felt really good. So good in fact I was really tempted to push things and go for 10 mins or so. But self control set in and I stopped at 5 mins.

I know I can easily do more from a cardio perspective, but I don't want to rush things. SOOOO many times I've got the idea of getting running again into my head, got over eager and ended up injured or knackered.
