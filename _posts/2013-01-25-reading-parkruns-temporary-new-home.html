Just spotted this on Twitter...

Reading parkrun is temporarily moving to Dinton Pastures Country Park, RG10 0TH from 2nd Feb 2013 till further notice. parkrun.org.uk/reading/course/

— Reading parkrun (@Readingparkrun) January 25, 2013

Woohoo, good to see Reading parkrun has a temporary new home, though I'm very surprised it has taken flooding in TVP to get a parkrun at Dinton Pastures. I would have thought Woodley parkrun would have jumped at the opportunity of being based out of Dinton Pastures than out of the teeny weeny little Woodford Park in the middle of Woodley.
